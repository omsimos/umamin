# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2024 

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Umamin! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our open-source platform for sending and receiving encrypted anonymous messages.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

- **Authentication:** When you sign up or log in to Umamin, we collect your username and password. Passwords are hashed and securely stored.
- **Google OAuth:** If you choose to authenticate using Google OAuth, we collect basic profile information from your Google account, such as your email address and profile picture. We use this to avoid account loss, display your profile picture, and show your connected account's email in settings.

### 2.2 Usage Data

- **Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our platform. This includes:
  - Number of users
  - Session statistics
  - Browser and device information
